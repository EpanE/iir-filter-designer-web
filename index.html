How to use — IIR Speech Filter

Quick start

  1. Plug in headphones to avoid feedback.
  2. Click Start Mic and allow microphone access.
  3. Choose settings:
    • Enable IIR Filter, Lowpass Only (optional), Apply Notch (50/60 Hz).
    • HP/LP Order: 2/4/6/8 (cascaded biquads).
    • Show Phase Response to view unwrapped phase.
  4. Use presets: Telephone (300–3400 Hz) or Podcast (80–8000 Hz).

Recording & saving

  1. Click Start Recording (records RAW and FILTERED).
  2. Stop Recording → Save Recordings to download WAV files.
  3. WAV rate: Auto (native) or 16,000 Hz (resampled) for speech.

Troubleshooting

  • No audio? Ensure mic permission and that the tab isn’t muted.
  • Echo/feedback? Use headphones, lower speaker volume.
  • Notch not effective? Confirm mains (50 vs 60 Hz) and increase Q.
